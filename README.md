## Bio

Craig Jahnke is Technical Architect at Microsoft and dedicated to helping everyday people adopt AI confidently and responsibly.

For two decades, He has helped enterprise organizations modernize their technology, build intelligent workflows, and navigate digital transformation. Along the way, Craig has discovered a consistent pattern:
Success isn't achieved with just technology and theory, but more with clarity, mindset, and practical application.

Craig's focuses on:
- Translating complex AI concepts into simple, actionable workflows
- Teaching teams how to save 5–10 hours a week using AI tools
- Designing AI‑powered processes for operations, sales, HR, and customer service
- Delivering keynotes and workshops that demystify AI for non‑technical audiences

He believe AI should empower people, not intimidate them.

He mission is to make AI accessible, practical, and human — especially for those who feel left behind by the hype.
